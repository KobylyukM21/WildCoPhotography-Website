About Me

Hi I’m Emily!

Since I will be asking you a million questions to get to know you, I figured you'd like to know some stuff about me too! So, let me tell you a little bit more about myself… I’m a 22 year old girl who was born in Philadelphia PA. I was raised in Florida and moved to Ohio when I was 15 years old. Ever since I got my first phone I took pictures of everything- and I mean EVERYTHING. It wasn’t until later, after I settled down in Ohio, I realized I had a passion for photography and it blossomed from there! This started off as a hobby for me. I was taking photos of my friends and family and I realized I wanted to do this forever. Since then, God has blessed me with so many opportunities to grow further in my business. It is all because of him!
